About VRTour Platform

Welcome to VRTour, your premier destination for exploring the world from the comfort of your browser. As an academic project, our mission is to demonstrate the potential of WebGL and lightweight 3D environments to revolutionize the travel booking experience.

Our Mission

To allow users to virtually explore destinations through a browser-based 3D experience before deciding to travel there. By offering a "try before you buy" approach to tourism, we aim to build excitement and confidence in travel decisions.

Technology Stack

This platform was built using HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, Three.js for 3D rendering, and a robust Java Spring Boot backend with a MySQL database.
