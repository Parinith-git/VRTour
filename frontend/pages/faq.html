Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a VR Headset?

No, our platform runs entirely within your web browser. You can seamlessly use your mouse and keyboard on a desktop, or touch controls on mobile devices to explore destinations.

How do I navigate in the VR environment?

On desktop, click anywhere on the VR screen to lock your mouse (allowing you to look around freely) and use the W, A, S, D keys to walk. On mobile devices, utilize the intuitive virtual joystick on the bottom left and drag the screen to look around.

Is this a real booking platform?

No, VRTour is a conceptual academic prototype project. No real payments are processed, and the destinations and travel packages provided are for demonstration purposes only.
